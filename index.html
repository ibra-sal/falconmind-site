How It Works

From Assessment to Action
in Minutes

A complete clinical workflow — soldier completes, AI interprets, clinician acts. No manual coordination, no paperwork.

1
Soldier selects population
Pre-entry, Active duty, or Veteran. The correct battery of assessments loads automatically — no admin setup needed.
2
Completes assessments online
Self-administered. Arabic or English. 20–30 minutes. Works on mobile, tablet, or desktop. No clinician in the room required.
3
Scores calculated instantly
All 44 assessments scored automatically using validated clinical algorithms. Severity labels and red/amber/green flags assigned in real time.
4
AI generates clinical summary
The AI reads all scores together, identifies patterns, flags safety concerns, and writes a full narrative report tailored to military context — in Arabic or English.
"This soldier shows probable PTSD (PCL-5: 38) with co-occurring severe depression (PHQ-9: 19) and significant sleep disruption. Burnout levels are elevated. Recommended: IES-R, DERS-16 for deeper analysis."
5
Clinician reviews flagged cases
Only flagged soldiers surface in the clinician dashboard. Full scores, AI summary, and question-by-question responses all in one view. Clinician makes the clinical decision.
6
One click — assign deeper tools
AI suggests which additional assessments would sharpen the clinical picture. Clinician clicks send — soldier receives them instantly. Results flow back automatically.
